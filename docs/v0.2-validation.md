This validates the v0.2 target:

- Keep the v0.1 transport and volume control features working.

- Confirm root browse, album hierarchy browse and item drill-down.
- Keep search, queue, playlists, MCP, auth and OpenAI out of scope.

UTF-8 output:

## Notes

Roon browse `item_key` values are session/list scoped and can change between browse calls. Use the `item_key` from the immediately previous response, or keep a stable `session_key` across related browse calls.
